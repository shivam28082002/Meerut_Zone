Facts about Meerut

The Revolt of 1857

Meerut played a significant role during the Indian Rebellion of 1857, also known as the First War of Independence.

Sporting Heritage

Meerut is known as the “Sports City of India” because of its contribution to the field of sports.

Educational Hub

Meerut is home to several prestigious educational institutions, including Chaudhary Charan Singh University.

Connection to Mahabharata

According to Hindu mythology, Meerut is closely associated with the epic tale of Mahabharata. It is believed to be the birthplace of Draupadi.
